# 🔍 Spot Check · S1 HealthTech

**Сфера:** S · **Квест:** S1

## 🎯 Мета
Health app claim «FDA approved» — але це ** wellness app**, не medical device.

## 📋 Сценарій
App store: «FDA approved algorithm!» Fine print: FDA registered facility for food packaging, not the AI diagnostic feature.

## 🕵️ Твоє завдання
1. Decode the claim.
2. User harm if trusted?
3. One question for founder.

## ⚠️ Пастка
Word «FDA» has many meanings.
