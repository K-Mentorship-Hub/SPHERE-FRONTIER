# Play Layer

## What This Is

A **lightweight entry layer** before Mission Lab, sphere quests, or deep repos.

- **One metaphor per card** — no mixed images (no robots + musicians + conductors in one card).
- **~30 seconds** to understand the map.
- **Then** → Mission Lab, fork, or a sphere repo.

Play cards live in [`../quests/play/`](../quests/play/). Machine-readable index: [`../quests/play/manifest.json`](../quests/play/manifest.json).

## Two Layers In The Hub

| Layer | Purpose | Where |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **Play** | motivation, one clear picture | `quests/play/*.md`, dashboard **Play Intro** |
| **Mission Lab** | mode, deliverable, checklist, twist | `dashboard.html` |

Do not replace Mission Lab with Play cards. Play is step 0.
